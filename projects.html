SetStory

For the 2015 SXSW Music Hackathon Championship, I built SetStory, the ultimate festival analytics and exploration platform. This product is targeted towards towards music festival planners, estimating ticket sales given artists, and providing a value algorithm to determine the ideal artists for a given festival.

GiveHub

At HackCU 2015, I built GiveHub with two of my best friends. GiveHub is charity powered by Twitter. With simply a tweet, you are able to donate to any charity, by handle, that you care about. GiveHub is built on the Twitter stream API and uses NLP to automatically generate a link with how much the individual says they will donate. All it takes is a retweet to match a friend's donation.
